🚛 10-MINUTE LOAD SOP

Service Efficiency Standard Operating Procedure

10:00
Maximum Load Time Target
1
Pre-Arrival Preparation
0:00 - 2:00
  • Batch ticket reviewed and verified before truck arrival
  • Materials staged and hoppers confirmed ready
  • Mixer truck inspection completed (cleanliness check)
  • Communication with driver on load specifications
2
Loading Sequence
2:00 - 7:00
  • Truck positioned under batch plant
  • Batch initiated immediately upon positioning
  • Water and admixtures added per specification
  • Aggregate and cement loaded in proper sequence
  • Drum rotation confirmed at proper RPM
3
Final Verification & Dispatch
7:00 - 10:00
  • Load ticket printed and verified
  • Slump test performed if required
  • Driver briefed on delivery instructions
  • Truck cleared for departure
  • Time logged in dispatch system

⚠️ Critical Standards

  • Any load exceeding 10 minutes requires immediate supervisor notification
  • Equipment failures must be reported and logged before end of shift
  • No truck leaves without completed inspection checklist
  • Customer wait time is our #1 service metric
