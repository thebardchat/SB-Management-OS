🧹 CLEANLINESS STANDARDS

Zero-Tolerance Standard Operating Procedure

ZERO
Tolerance Policy

📋 Daily Cleanliness Standards

Truck Fleet

All mixer trucks washed and drums cleaned before end of each shift. No dried concrete buildup tolerated.

Batch Plant

Control room, scales, and loading area swept and organized. Spillage cleaned immediately.

Yard & Grounds

Material piles contained, drainage clear, no debris accumulation. Professional appearance at all times.

Break Areas

Employee areas kept clean, trash disposed properly, refrigerator cleaned weekly.
