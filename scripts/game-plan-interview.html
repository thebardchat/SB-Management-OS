🎯 GAME PLAN INTERVIEW

Structured Interview Script for Elite Hiring

Interview Objective

Identify candidates who embody our core values: Service, Cleanliness, Labor, and Leadership. This structured approach ensures consistent evaluation and helps identify top performers who will thrive in our culture.

1
Opening & Rapport Building
5 mins
Opening Script

"Thank you for coming in today. I'm [Name], and I'll be conducting your interview. At SRM, we're looking for people who want to be part of the best ready-mix provider in North Alabama. Before we dive in, do you have any questions about the role or our company?"

💡 Interviewer Tips

  • Observe punctuality - did they arrive early?
  • Note their appearance and presentation
  • Watch for enthusiasm and engagement
2
Core Value Assessment
20 mins
  • SERVICE: "Tell me about a time you went above and beyond for a customer or coworker. What was the situation and what did you do?"
  • CLEANLINESS: "How do you maintain your workspace and equipment? Give me a specific example of your standards."
  • LABOR: "Describe your work ethic. What does 'working hard' look like to you on a daily basis?"
  • LEADERSHIP: "Tell me about a time you took initiative without being asked. What did you see that needed to be done?"
Follow-up Prompt

"Our mantra is 'If you aren't busy doing something, get busy!' How does that resonate with you?"

3
Experience & Skills
15 mins
  • "Walk me through your experience with [relevant equipment/vehicles]. What's your comfort level?"
  • "What safety certifications do you hold? How do you prioritize safety in your daily work?"
  • "Describe a challenging situation at a previous job. How did you handle it?"
  • "What hours are you available to work? Are you flexible with scheduling?"
4
Scenario Questions
10 mins
  • "A customer is upset about a late delivery. How do you handle the situation?"
  • "You notice a coworker cutting corners on a safety procedure. What do you do?"
  • "It's end of shift and there's still work to be done. How do you respond?"
5
Closing
5 mins
Closing Script

"Do you have any questions for me about the position, our team, or our company? ... Thank you for your time today. We'll be making our decision within [timeframe] and will be in touch. Is there anything else you'd like us to know about you?"

🚩 Red Flags to Watch For

  • Negative comments about previous employers
  • Vague or evasive answers about work history
  • Lack of specific examples when asked
  • No questions about the role or company
  • Poor presentation or hygiene
  • Arrives late without good explanation
